Akan-naming culture of Ghana

About Akan naming

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name'
which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are
Ghanian day names.

To demonstrate how it works I have created a web application
that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week
they were born and then depending on their gender outputs
their Akan Name.
